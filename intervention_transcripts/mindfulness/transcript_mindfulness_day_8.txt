hello and welcome to the first day of your second week
during this second week we will still keep to our basic structure
but we will also try out something new every day
the past week you have been taking the sensation of the breath in the nose as your anchor
but there are also other things that you can rest your attention on
it can be really pleasant to stay with the breath once the mind has calmed down a bit
but it can also be really pleasant or interesting to stay with other things too
today we will do that with sound
we will first stay with the breath and then switch in the end to taking sound as our main focus
so first let us check if you are sitting comfortably
feet on the floor
hands on your knees
spine straight
and relaxed
bring the way the breath feels in your belly into your awareness
and take a couple of deep breaths
relaxing on every outbreath
now let us bring the spot in the nose into your awareness where you feel the breath most right now
and once you have done that you can start counting
from one to ten then we start again from one
if your mind wandered take the opportunity to practice noticing and letting go
back to the breath
and for a little while we will say in with the inbreath and out with the outbreath
if your attention is not with the breath in the nose just take a moment to appreciate whatever sensation thought or experience came into your awareness
and slowly let it go and come back to the breath
now we will not say in and out anymore we will just stay with the breath
remember to not lock your attention there try to see how long your attention will naturally stay there in this particular moment
gently noticing any distractions let us now bring our awareness to the sounds around you
all the tiny sounds of the place that you are in
maybe your neighbors maybe things happening outside maybe you can hear animals people music a fridge construction
maybe they are loud maybe it is very quiet
can you hear your own body
can you hear your clothes as you make tiny movements to stay in balance
maybe you have thoughts that have a certain sound to them
maybe you hear some sounds on this recording of my voice
try to not identify any specific sound and where it comes from
but try to listen to the soundscape as a whole
everything together
everything that you can hear right now in your experience of this moment
as with the breath we are not forcing our attention here
we try to just sit back relax and watch as sounds come and go
not bringing this very serious work attitude to this but more a gentle playful relaxed curiosity
remember no need to be aware of or track every single sound
no need to name anything or understand what the source is of the sound
just focus on the sensory quality of the sound itself
like listening to an orchestra instead of the instruments that make up the orchestra
let sounds come to you not you to them
let them come and go as they are in this moment not judging
if you are distracted no worries that is a good opportunity to practice noticing accepting letting go
bring your personal soundscape back into your awareness
what is it like to have ears where you are at this moment
what does it sound like
all right now i hope you found this interesting
this is one you can really try in many different places and it might surprise you how nice it can be to not listen to anything in particular while still attentively listening
if it was not your thing no worries we will try something else tomorrow

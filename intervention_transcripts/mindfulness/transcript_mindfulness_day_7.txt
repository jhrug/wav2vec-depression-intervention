and congratulations on getting here
and i would suggest to begin by placing your hands on your knees
closing your eyes
keeping your feet flat on the floor
and your spine straight
check whether you are in a comfortable and stable position
relaxed and attentive
make adjustments if needed
taking a few deep breaths through the nose long and slow
feeling the belly filling on each inhale and clearing on each exhale
try to hold your breath for a bit before you breathe out
and let your outbreath be a little longer than your inbreath
relaxing your muscles on the way out
and now totally release control of the breath
try to let your body do its own thing
gently observing not needing to change anything or do anything special right now
letting the breath continue on its own we focus our attention at the opening of the nose
noticing how the air flows in and out
and how little effort you have to put in for that breathing to happen
and let this be our base where we go to when our mind wanders
as you are following along let us start counting until ten
then starting over from one
now we continue without counting
following the breath
saying in and out in your mind
in
out
if your mind has wandered away from the breath no worries
just softly go back to the breath
in
out
now let us rest our attention at the flow of the breath through the nose without words
feeling that sensation as it comes
feeling the cool air going in and warm air going out
sooner or later you lose focus
remember this is what human minds do
try to practice this radical movement of complete acceptance
acknowledging whatever came to us thanking it for visiting our consciousness
gently releasing it to reorient ourselves to the breath
letting whatever thought feeling sensation wants to come and visit just come and go
not letting ourselves be dragged into anything
staying here at the center
watching things come by
now when you are ready slowly open your eyes
noticing how you feel compared to when you started
and as always just be aware of the fact that today you showed up for yourself
and that is something to thank yourself for
